Data Bias Assoicated with Stasis in Ancient Greece
Stasis appears more frequently in the historical record in poleis with higher prominence.
Higher prominence indicates that more information is recorded about a given Pollis, including information about Stasis.
This could create a bias in the historical record and influence the way that we study Stosis.
