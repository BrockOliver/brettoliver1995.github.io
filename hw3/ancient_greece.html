Data Bias Assoicated with Stasis in Ancient Greece
In this dataset Stasis can only be observed where there is a record of it. This creates bias in the record that attributes stasis
to polleis with higher prominence. We should see that Stasis and prominence have a positive correlation, which is tested with
a histogram and r-squared value of polleis vs stasis.

Data obtained from Professor Scott Arcenas of the University of Montana History Department
